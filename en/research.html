Research

We focus on practical and rigorous methods that bridge algorithm design, systems implementation, and real-world deployment.

Reliable AI Systems

Methods for uncertainty-aware inference, distribution-shift monitoring, and failure-resilient model serving pipelines in production environments.

Cloud-Edge Intelligence

Scheduling and orchestration strategies for latency-sensitive learning workloads across cloud, edge, and on-device resources.

Privacy & Data Infrastructure

Privacy-preserving analytics, secure collaboration, and scalable storage systems for data-centric scientific and industrial applications.

Human-Centered AI Engineering

Interface and workflow design that enables domain experts to inspect, steer, and trust AI-assisted decision systems.
