## missionTitle
Our Mission

## description
Bae Lab's research is structured around three complementary domains that connect material innovation with practical implementation.

## areaLabel
Research Area

## cards
- Printing Technology | We develop advanced additive manufacturing strategies and AI-assisted printable inks to fabricate high-performance functional systems that are difficult to realize with conventional methods. | printing-tech
- Energy & Environmental Technology | We design functional polymers and chemical materials for next-generation energy harvesters, lithium metal batteries, environmental sensors, and carbon-capture related platforms. | energy-environment-tech
- Bio Technology | We engineer soft and functional materials for biosensors, bioelectronics, and soft robotic applications requiring compliant, high-performance interfaces. | biomedical-tech

## methodsTitle
Research Methodology

## methods
- Materials Design and Synthesis | Molecular and formulation-level design of functional materials tailored for additive manufacturing.
- Additive Manufacturing and Process Engineering | Process-window optimization, rheology control, and scalable patterning for robust device fabrication.
- Application Validation and Data-Driven Optimization | Electrochemical, mechanical, and biological performance evaluation coupled with data-guided feedback loops.
